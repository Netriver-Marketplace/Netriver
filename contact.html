Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a seller?

To register as a seller, click on the "Register" button in the navigation menu and fill out the registration form with your business details. You'll need to provide your business name, contact information, and business address.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through Paystack, which supports various payment methods including debit/credit cards, bank transfers, USSD, and QR codes. All transactions are secure and processed in Nigerian Naira.

How much commission do you charge?

We charge a 10% commission on all successful sales. This means you keep 90% of the sale price. The commission is automatically calculated and deducted when an order is completed.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard security measures including password hashing, SSL encryption, and secure session management to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties.

How do I track my orders?

After placing an order, you'll receive an order number. You can use this number to track your order status on our platform. Sellers also update order statuses as they process and ship your items.

Can I sell anywhere in Nigeria?

Yes! We serve all 36 states plus the Federal Capital Territory. Sellers can ship to customers across Nigeria, and buyers can purchase from sellers located anywhere in the country.
