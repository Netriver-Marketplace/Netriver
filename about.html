About Netriver

Learn more about our mission and values

Our Story

Netriver was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a secure, trustworthy, and efficient online marketplace specifically designed for the Nigerian market. We recognized the need for a platform that connects genuine sellers with quality-conscious buyers across Nigeria.

Our journey began with a deep understanding of the challenges faced by both buyers and sellers in the Nigerian e-commerce landscape. We saw sellers struggling to reach customers and buyers hesitant to trust online transactions. Netriver was born to bridge this gap.

Our Mission

At Netriver, our mission is to empower Nigerian businesses by providing a secure and accessible platform to showcase their products to a wider audience. We believe in fostering economic growth through digital commerce while maintaining the highest standards of security and trust.

We are committed to creating an environment where sellers can thrive and buyers can shop with confidence, knowing they are dealing with verified businesses and protected by robust security measures.

Our Commitment to Security

Security is at the core of everything we do at Netriver. We implement multiple layers of protection to ensure a safe environment for all our users:

  • Password Hashing: All passwords are securely hashed using bcrypt, ensuring they can never be compromised.
  • SQL Injection Protection: We use parameterized queries to prevent SQL injection attacks.
  • Rate Limiting: Our rate limiting system prevents brute force attacks on login and API endpoints.
  • Session Management: Secure session handling with HTTP-only cookies protects user sessions.
  • Input Validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized to prevent XSS and other attacks.
  • CSRF Protection: Cross-site request forgery protection safeguards user actions.
